Help / Instructions

Select your sex, race goal, injury history, and goal race pace. The application will generate a personalized 8-week training plan. If you choose “prone to injury,” mileage is reduced by 1 mile where possible.

  • WU = Warm-Up
  • CD = Cool-Down
  • GRP = Goal Race Pace
  • Strides = Short fast controlled runs
