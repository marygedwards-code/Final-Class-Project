Help / Instructions

Select your sex, race goal, injury history, current mileage, and goal race pace. The app will generate an 8-week training plan. If you choose “prone to injury,” daily mileage is reduced to make the plan safer.

  • WU means warm-up.
  • CD means cool-down.
  • GRP means goal race pace.
  • Strides are short controlled fast runs.
